Presentation :

This is a fan project where I'm showcasing the Path Of Exile items I made using Path of Exile Item Creator.

For each item I present the core gameplay intentions and a comment about the overall flavor of the item.

These items use art from the game Path of Exile, I took the images from the game's wiki.

Most of these items are made as jewels but should probably be adapted to other item types,
they may also be unbalanced, but the focus should be on the concept and flavor of the item.

Some items are edgy but hopefully just the right amount.
